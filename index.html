About Farm Eleven O'Clock

Wildlife Breeding & Livestock Breeding Operations

Established and operated by the A.V. Duma Family Trust, our private ranch spans over 1,000 hectares of natural wilderness. We operate two distinct farming enterprises: Wildlife Breeding for trophy hunting and conservation, and Livestock Breeding for sustainable agricultural production with premium African breeds.
